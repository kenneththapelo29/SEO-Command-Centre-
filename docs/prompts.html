Prompt Library

Copy and paste these into Claude Code after fetching your data. Each one is ready to use.

Open Claude Code in your project directory. It reads CLAUDE.md automatically. These prompts work best when data has been fetched within the last 30 days.

Paid vs Organic

💰 Find wasted ad spend GSC + Ads

The highest-value analysis. Finds every keyword where you are paying for clicks you could get for free.

Compare data/gsc/queries.json against data/ads/search_terms.json. Find keywords where we are paying for ad clicks but already rank in positions 1-5 organically. These are candidates to pause or reduce paid spend. Also find keywords where we are spending on ads with zero organic presence. Those are content gaps we should be writing about. Output a prioritised table for each category sorted by spend.
💡 Run this first. It almost always finds immediate savings.
📈 Paid amplification candidates GSC + Ads

Find organic winners that paid could amplify for even more visibility.

Read data/gsc/queries.json and data/ads/search_terms.json. Find the top 20 organic queries by impression volume that we are not currently running ads against. These are amplification candidates where paid support would compound our organic presence.

Ranking Opportunities

🎯 Page 2 to page 1 quick wins GSC

Queries on the edge of page 1. A content refresh could move them up.

Read data/gsc/queries.json. Find queries with average position between 8 and 20 and impressions over 500. These are our easiest ranking wins. Sort by impression volume and show current CTR alongside position.
💡 Position 8-12 queries are often one content update away from page 1.
📊 Topic cluster analysis GSC

Group all queries by topic to find where to invest in content.

Read data/gsc/queries.json. Group all queries into topic clusters based on semantic similarity and intent. For each cluster show total impressions, average position and top 3 queries. Which clusters have the most traffic potential but worst average position?

Content and UX

📉 Low CTR high impression pages GSC

Pages that show in search but don't get clicked. Title or meta description problem.

Read data/gsc/queries.json. Find queries in positions 1-10 with over 500 impressions but CTR below 3 percent. These pages are showing but not getting clicked. Rank by impression volume. These need title tag or meta description improvements.
⚠️ High bounce organic pages GSC + GA4

Pages that rank but disappoint. Visitors arrive and leave immediately.

Read data/ga4/channel_traffic.json and data/gsc/pages.json. Find pages that rank in GSC positions 1-10 but have bounce rates above 70 percent in GA4. Sort by traffic volume multiplied by bounce rate. These pages need content improvement or better UX.

Lead and Conversion Analysis

💬 Lead source analysis GSC + Forms

Connect your traffic to actual leads. Requires Google Forms fetcher.

Read data/forms/all_leads.json and data/gsc/queries.json. Which organic keywords are driving the most form submissions? Cross-reference by page URL. Which pages convert traffic into leads and which do not?
💰 Cost per lead Ads + Forms

The real ROI question. What does each lead actually cost?

Read data/forms/all_leads.json and data/ads/search_terms.json. Calculate cost per lead from paid traffic. Which campaigns are generating leads versus burning budget? Compare paid cost per lead against organic cost per lead.

AI Visibility

🤖 AI citation analysis AI + GSC

Are you showing up in AI search? Are your pages competing against each other?

Read data/ai-visibility/bing_copilot.json and data/gsc/pages.json. Which pages are being cited in AI responses? Are any pages competing for the same AI citations on the same topics? Flag cannibalization risks. Show citation count alongside GSC position and click volume.

Reports

📋 Full monthly SEO report All sources

One prompt. Complete client-ready report.

Read all JSON files in data/gsc/, data/ga4/, data/ads/, data/forms/ and data/ai-visibility/. Also read config.json for client context. Write a comprehensive SEO report with these sections: 1. Executive Summary — 3 to 5 bullet points, most important findings 2. Organic Performance — top queries, top pages 3. Paid vs Organic Overlap — gap analysis summary 4. Content Opportunities — quick wins, topic gaps, high bounce pages 5. Lead Performance — form submissions by source 6. AI Visibility — citation presence and cannibalization risks 7. Recommended Actions — prioritised list, maximum 10 items Save the report to reports/report-YYYY-MM-DD.md
💡 Always verify numbers against source JSON before sending to a client.
